Citizen and Resident Platform

An independent platform that the citizen or resident can register with by identity, email or mobile and can enter his account and communicate with all parties from one place easily and easily through the creation of a new transaction and then specify the entity to communicate with, where the program selects the competent employee for this entity And it becomes an open transaction between the employee and the citizen to exchange opinions, suggestions and instructions in an easy and easy step
